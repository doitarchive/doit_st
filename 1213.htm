Matgraph C110ZV




Anschlußbelegung lt. Matrix-Handbuch für Matgraph C110ZV:

Pin 1 Rot (Analog)
Pin 2 Grün (Analog)
Pin 3 Blau (Analog)
Pin 4 H-Sync (TTL)
Pin 5 V-Sync (TTL)
Pin 6-9 GND

Mein Adapter ist folgendermaßen belegt: 

Matrix  Monitor
1       1
2       2
3       3
4       13
5       14

8       8

Pin 4,9,11,12 und 15 an der DB 15 Buchse des Adapters sind nicht belegt. Am Monitorstecker fehlt nur Pin 9 (Nicht benutzt).



Tech information on a range of Matrix cards (dated February 1991)

MATGRAPH C32

For Mega-ST 

Power supply:   PIN-1 +5V (red)
                PIN-2 +5V (red)
                PIN-3 GND (blk)
                PIN-4 GND (blk)
                PIN-5 GND (blk)
                PIN-6 +12V (blu) not needed

9-pin Socket:   Pin-1 red (analog)
                Pin-2 grn (analog)
                Pin-3 blu (analog)
                Pin-4 H-sync (TTL)
                Pin-5 V-sync (TTL)
                Pin-6 GND
                Pin-7 GND
                Pin-8 GND
                Pin-9 GND


Videofreq 28/25/16 MHz
Video RAM 256 kB, max 1 Mb DRAM
Processor Intel 82786
FPU socket available, MC68881 16 MHz optional
Pal 6-bit, 256 col from 262144, 64 greyscales standard
8-bit, 256 col from 16.7 mil, 256 greyscales optional
Power cons. +5V 500 mA

Bus system: Mega ST expansion bus



MATGRAPH C110 and C110Z

For Mega-ST

Power supply: see C32

9-pin Socket: see C32, pin 2 = analog with composite sync

Videofreq 125/110/64/32/16 MHZ
Video RAM 1 Mb, max 2 Mb VRAM
Processor Intel 82786
FPU socket, MC68881 16 MHZ optional
Pal 8-bit, 256 col from 16.7 mil, 256 greyscales
Power cons. +5V 800 mA, +12V 200 mA



MATGRAPH CoCo (color controller)

For Mega STE or TT (VME)

9-pin socket: see C32

Videofreq 50/28/25/16 MHz Note that later versions could have 50/28.8/25/14.4/12.5 MHz
Video RAM 1 Mb
Processor Intel 82786
Pal 6-bit 256 col from 262144, 64 greyscales standard
8-bit 256 col from 16.7 mil, 256 greyscales optional
Power cons. +5V 850 mA, +12V not used



MATGRAPH MoCo (Monochrome controller)

For Mega STE or TT (VME) 

9-pin plug:     pin-1 Video+ (ECL)
                pin-2 GND
                pin-3 HSync- (TTL)
                pin-4 VSync- (TTL)
                pin-5 free
                pin-6 Video- (ECL)
                pin-7 GND
                pin-8 GND
                pin-9 free


Videofreq 110 MHz standard, 125 or 160 MHz optional
Video RAM 256 kB
Processor Intel 82786
Power cons +5V 850 mA, +12V 350 mA



MATGRAPH MiCo (mixed controller)

For Mega STE or TT (VME)

9-pin socket for color screens, see CoCo
9-pin plug for mono systems, see MoCo

Videofreq 110(125)/50/28/25/16 MHZ
Video RAM 1 Mb
Processor Intel 82786
Pal 6-bit 256 col from 262144, 64 greyscales standard
8-bit 256 col from 16.7 mil, 256 greyscales optional
Power cons +5V 850 mA, +12V 320 mA
